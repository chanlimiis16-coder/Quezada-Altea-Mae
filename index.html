Altea Mae M. Quezada

Proud Tingguian | BEED Student

Hi! I am Altea Mae M. Quezada, a proud Tingguian and a BEED student who dreams of becoming a teacher that inspires and nurtures. I currently live in Consolado, Lingtan, Bangued, Abra, but my permanent home is in Naglibacan, Tineg, Abra, a place that shaped who I am today.

I was born on April 11, 2005, to my loving parents, Rhey Louis Quezada and Carolina Mariano Quezada, who taught me the values of hard work, perseverance, and kindness. Growing up in a close-knit community, I learned the importance of culture, family, and staying true to one’s roots.

I am passionate about teaching because I believe every child deserves guidance, encouragement, and a safe space to grow. I want to be the kind of teacher who not only shares knowledge but also inspires confidence, curiosity, and love for learning in every student I meet.

When I’m not studying, I enjoy reflecting on life, exploring new ideas, and thinking of creative ways to make learning fun and meaningful. My journey as a future educator is just beginning, but I am ready to embrace every challenge and opportunity with a big heart and an open mind.
“In progress, but with purpose.”
